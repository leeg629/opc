Octopus

An octopus, a sea animal with eight legs, known to change color and release

ink. Generally depicted as a pink or orange octopus facing forward,with

a large, round head, prominent eyes, and its tentacle-like arms raised

at its sides. Platforms vary in the number of arms shown, ranging from

four to the full eight, with several designs featuring suckers on them.
