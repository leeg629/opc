gorilla

Gorillas are ground-dwelling, predominantly herbivorous apes that inhabit the forest of central Sub-Saharan Africa. They are the largest living primates, and yes, you may think of King-Kong.

chimpanzee

The chimpanzee, is a species of great ape native to the forest and savannah of tropical Africa. They are our closest living relatives.

monkey

Monkeys make up two of the three groups of simian primates, Old World monkeys and New World monkeys. Monkeys express affection and make peace with others by grooming each other.
